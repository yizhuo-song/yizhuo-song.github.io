About Us

健康加油站于2020年在深圳创立，面向但不局限于慢性病及亚健康患者及潜在患者人群提供集中式健康餐饮服务。健康加油站多年致力于研究高血压、高血脂、糖尿病等慢性病的饮食改善方法，自主研发了多种菜品可供选择。吃的美味、吃的健康是我们品牌的服务宗旨。
Health Station was established in Shenzhen in 2020, providing centralized healthy catering services for but not limited to chronic disease and sub-health patients and potential patients. For many years, the Health Gas Station has been committed to researching dietary improvement methods for chronic diseases such as hypertension, hyperlipidemia, and diabetes, and has independently developed a variety of dishes to choose from. Delicious and healthy eating are the service tenet of our brand.

健康加油站分为线下服务和线上点餐两个功能模块。线下区域包含备餐区、就餐区、宣传区和功能区，为顾客提供最为舒适和健康的就餐环境。
Health Station is divided into two functional modules: offline service and online ordering. The offline area includes a meal preparation area, a dining area, a publicity area and a functional area, providing customers with the most comfortable and healthy dining environment.

线上模块包含问卷调查、调查报告、饮食推荐和习惯激励等功能，为用户量身打造膳食搭配均衡营养，追踪用户饮食与身体近况，激励用户养成健康饮食健康生活的好习惯。
The online module includes functions such as questionnaire surveys, survey reports, diet recommendations and habit incentives, tailor-made meals with balanced nutrition for users, track users' diet and body conditions, and encourage users to develop good habits of healthy eating and healthy living.



健康加油站从To B的商业模式开始做起，主要面向互联网企业餐厅，聚焦IT从业者的多种身体状况，为他们提供集中式健康饮食服务。在未来，将推广到更多的应用场景，并做成特色健康餐厅，为消费者提供更加最佳的健康饮食体验。 

Health Station started from the To B business model, which mainly provides service for Internet corporate restaurants, focusing on the various physical conditions of IT practitioners, and providing them with centralized healthy eating services. In the future, it will be promoted to more application scenarios and made into a characteristic healthy restaurant to provide consumers with a more optimal healthy eating experience.
